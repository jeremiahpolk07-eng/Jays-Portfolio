Founder & developer

I'm NOT your typical Computer Science student, and I NEVER planned to be.

Born in Portsmouth, Virginia and now based in Atlanta, I'm a 20-year-old creative and tech-driven mind currently studying Computer Science at Voorhees University. While I'm building my path toward becoming a professional software developer, my story doesn't just live behind a screen - it moves, performs, and creates in real life.

From being dancer and choreographer who has performed with numerous artists, turning ideas into energy on stage. That same drive carries into athletics competing in track and field, specializing in the 400m hurdles, 110m hurdles, and 800m, where discipline, endurance, and precision matter just as much as talent.

What makes me different is how I blend worlds. I can go from performing on stage to solving problems in code, from creative direction to technical execution. I understand both structure and expression - and I use that balance to stand out in every space I enter.

At the core, I'm a builder. Whether it's choreography, a personal brand, or future software, I create with intention and this is just the beginning.
