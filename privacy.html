Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information SZN STACKZ collects through contact and onboarding forms, how that information is used, and how it is protected.

Information Collected

SZN STACKZ may collect your name, email address, phone number, project details, uploaded files, and any other information you voluntarily submit through forms or direct communication.

How Information Is Used

  • To review project inquiries and respond to requests.
  • To prepare proposals, agreements, invoices, and project materials.
  • To communicate updates related to ongoing or requested services.

Data Sharing

Your information is not sold. It may be shared only with trusted service providers needed to deliver services, such as form processors, email providers, payment tools, hosting services, or deployment platforms.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect submitted information, but no internet-based transmission or storage system can be guaranteed as fully secure.

Your Choices

You may request correction or deletion of submitted personal information, subject to any legal, payment, or recordkeeping obligations that require retention.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. The most current version will remain available on this page.
